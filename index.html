FAQ

Quick guide to the core workflow: capture snapshots, add markups, and track effort with tags & hours.

How do I start?
Click Import or drop a video into the player. Your work is saved locally in your browser.
How do snapshots work?
Pause the video, then draw on the frame or add tags/hours. The app creates a snapshot at that timecode and shows it in the right sidebar.
How do I add markups?
Pick a tool (Draw / Rect / Circle / Arrow / Text) and draw on the video. Use Select to move/resize objects.
How do tags & hours work?
Click a tag to activate it (the hours field will focus). Type hours to estimate effort. Tags/hours are saved on the active snapshot and shown on the snapshot card.
Why do tags reset when I press play?
When playback resumes, the app exits edit mode and clears the tags/hours UI so you don’t accidentally edit a snapshot while watching.
How do I navigate snapshots?
Use the snapshot list on the right, or use your keyboard: up/down arrows move between snapshots in timeline order.
How do I delete a snapshot or project?
Press and hold the delete icon until the progress fill completes. Release early to cancel.
How do I share or export?
Share exports a project file. Export PDF generates a report with snapshots, tags/hours, and comments.
How do I edit the available tags?
Click Tags in the top bar to open the tag manager.
